FAQ

Questions?
We've got answers.

Is it free?

Yes, CueCard is completely free and open source. Check out the code on GitHub and give us a star!

Is CueCard undetectable during meetings?

Yes, it's designed to be undetectable on Zoom, Teams, Google Meet, and similar apps. Test it in a practice call first to ensure it works with your setup. Report Issues

Do I need a browser extension for Google Slides?

Yes, to sync notes from Google Slides you'll need the browser extension. Available for Safari, Chrome and Firefox.

Can I use CueCard without Google Slides?

Yes! You can type or paste notes directly into CueCard for any meeting. Google Slides sync is optional.

How do the timer and note tags work?

Add [time mm:ss] to set timing for each section, and [note name] to remind yourself of anything during the meet. Both are optional.

Can others see my notes during screen sharing?

No. Turn off 'Show in Screen Capture' option in Settings to hide CueCard from screen shares completely.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All your notes and Google tokens are stored locally on your device. Check our Privacy Policy for details.
