FAQ

Questions?

Is it free?

Yes, CueCard is completely free and open source. Check out the code at github.com/thisisnsh/cuecard and give us a star!

What platforms are supported?

Currently CueCard is available for macOS. Windows and Linux support coming soon.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All your notes and Google tokens are stored locally on your device. We only collect your name and email to know our users. Nothing else leaves your system. Check our Privacy Policy for details.
