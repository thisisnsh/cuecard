Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

At CueCard, we believe in transparency and respect for your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our service:

  • Name and Email Address: When you sign in with Google, we collect your name and email address to identify you as a user. This helps us understand who uses CueCard and enables us to provide support when needed.
  • Usage Patterns: We track count of times you pasted notes or synced google slide notes. This helps us understand why people use CueCard and improve the service.

That's it. We don't collect anything else.

Information We Don't Collect

We want to be crystal clear about what stays on your device:

  • Your Notes: All speaker notes you create or paste are stored locally on your device. They never leave your computer.
  • Google Tokens: Your Google authentication tokens are stored locally on your device. We don't have access to them.
  • Presentation Content: Any content synced from Google Slides remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

How We Store Your Information

The limited information we collect (name and email) is stored securely using Google Cloud infrastructure. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

How We Use Your Information

We use your name and email solely to:

  • Identify you as a CueCard user
  • Provide customer support when you contact us
  • Send important updates about the service (very rarely)

We will never sell your information to third parties or use it for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

CueCard integrates with Google services for authentication and Google Slides sync. When you use these features, you're also subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We only request the minimum permissions necessary to provide the functionality you need.

Data Retention

We retain your basic account information (name and email) for as long as you use CueCard. If you wish to have your information deleted, please contact us at support@cuecard.dev.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Security

We take security seriously. Your local data is protected by your operating system's security features. The information stored in our cloud infrastructure is protected using industry-standard encryption and security practices.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@cuecard.dev
